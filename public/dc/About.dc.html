01 / Who We Are

Twelve years.
Two hundred projects.

Relicsol is a web design and software development agency helping businesses across the USA, UK, and Europe build a stronger digital presence. We have been doing this since 2013, before AI automation was a buzzword, before headless CMS was mainstream, before most of our clients had ever heard of Core Web Vitals.

We combine strategy, design, development, and automation to deliver websites, custom software, SEO campaigns, and AI automation systems that are built for performance, not just appearance. Every project starts with understanding your business goals, your audience, and the outcomes you need.

We do not use off-the-shelf templates or one-size-fits-all solutions. Everything we build is crafted specifically for your business, your market, and your growth targets.
